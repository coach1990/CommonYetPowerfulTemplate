Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our pricing and features

Can I change my plan later? +

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes to your subscription will be prorated and reflected in your next billing cycle.

What happens after my free trial? +

After your 14-day free trial ends, your account will automatically switch to the plan you selected. We'll send you reminders before the trial ends, and you can cancel anytime before being charged.

Do you offer discounts for nonprofits or educational institutions? +

Yes, we offer special pricing for nonprofits, educational institutions, and startups. Please contact our sales team for more information about our discount programs.

Can I cancel my subscription at any time? +

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. If you cancel, you'll still have access to your plan until the end of your current billing cycle.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover) as well as PayPal. For Enterprise plans, we also offer invoicing options.
