Introduction to Horizon

Welcome to the Horizon documentation. Learn how to use our platform to streamline your team's workflow and boost productivity.

What is Horizon?

Horizon is a comprehensive collaboration platform designed to help teams work together more effectively, regardless of their location. Our platform combines project management, communication, file sharing, and more into a single, intuitive interface.

Whether you're a small startup or a large enterprise, Horizon provides the tools you need to organize your work, communicate with your team, and track progress toward your goals.

Key Features

Horizon offers a wide range of features to support your team's workflow:

  • Project Management: Create, organize, and track projects with customizable workflows.
  • Task Tracking: Assign tasks, set deadlines, and monitor progress in real-time.
  • Team Collaboration: Communicate with your team through integrated messaging and comments.
  • File Sharing: Store, share, and collaborate on files within the platform.
  • Calendar: Schedule meetings and deadlines with a shared team calendar.
  • Reporting: Generate insights with customizable reports and dashboards.
  • Integrations: Connect with your favorite tools through our extensive integration library.

Getting Started

The best way to get started with Horizon is to sign up for a free account and explore the platform. Our intuitive interface makes it easy to start creating projects and inviting team members right away.

For a guided introduction, check out our Quick Start Guide, which will walk you through the basics of setting up your workspace and creating your first project.

Note: Horizon offers a 14-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card required to get started.

System Requirements

Horizon is a cloud-based platform that works in any modern web browser. For the best experience, we recommend using the latest version of:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Microsoft Edge
  • Safari

Mobile apps are available for iOS and Android devices, providing access to key features on the go.

Support

If you need help with Horizon, there are several resources available:

  • Help Center: Browse our Help Center for detailed guides and tutorials.
  • Community: Connect with other users in our Community forum.
  • Support Team: Contact our support team directly through the platform or by emailing support@horizonapp.com.

Our support team is available Monday through Friday, 9am to 6pm EST. Enterprise customers have access to 24/7 priority support.
