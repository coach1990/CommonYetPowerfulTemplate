Our Mission is to Empower Teams

Our Story

Horizon began in 2018 when our founders, Alex and Sarah, experienced firsthand the challenges of managing distributed teams across different time zones. They were frustrated by the fragmented tools available and envisioned a unified platform that would make teamwork seamless regardless of location.

What started as a simple project management tool has evolved into a comprehensive platform used by thousands of teams worldwide. Our journey has been guided by a simple principle: make collaboration effortless so teams can focus on what matters most‚Äîcreating amazing work together.

Today, Horizon is a team of over 100 passionate individuals spread across 12 countries, all united by the mission to transform how teams work together in the digital age.
